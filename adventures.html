The Junior Nordic Program (JNP) is a cross-country skiing program for kids and teens age 4-18. It is operated out of the Summit Nordic Center at Snoqualmie Pass, WA. The JNP was started in the 1990s and has been teaching kids and teens freeheel skiing for 20+ years!

Ten Years of JNP Adventures

Each year JNP kids are called upon to use their skiing prowess, courage, and cleveness to save a magical world. We have helped elves, dwarves and Greek demi-gods and goddess. We have fought minotaurs, fog giants, dragons, snow spiders and beasts of all manner. We have used magical magnoto-beads to close magical doors to the other-worlds. We have jumped through the doors to Narnia and tracked Narnian wolves. We have rappelled into the underworld and done battle with Kronos. And on and on.
